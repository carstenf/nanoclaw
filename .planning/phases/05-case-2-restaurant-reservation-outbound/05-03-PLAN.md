Du bist in einem Detektions-Modus. Der Anruf wurde GERADE angenommen.

### VAD cadence gate (Bridge-side in amd-classifier.ts)

```
Timer A (armed at classifier start):
  Fires at 4000ms IF speech_started observed AND no speech_stopped yet AND no amd_result.
  Action: force verdict=voicemail (cadence cue — long uninterrupted speech = mailbox).

Timer B (armed at classifier start):
  Fires at 6000ms IF no speech_started observed AND no amd_result.
  Action: force verdict=voicemail (picked up but silent — also mailbox/error).

Either timer firing routes to voicemail hangup.
Both timers cleared on amd_result event.
```

### Outcome routing (Case-2 onCallEnd + reportBack)

- case_type=case_2, status=failed, error=voicemail_detected: schedule retry + notify info
- case_type=case_2, status=failed, error=line_busy: schedule retry + notify info
- case_type=case_2, status=failed, error=no_answer (30s ring timeout): schedule retry + notify info
- case_type=case_2, outcome=out_of_tolerance (persona ended call): notify decision with counter-offer, NO retry
- case_type=case_2, outcome=success (calendar entry created): notify info with summary
- case_type=case_2, daily_cap_reached (from voice_case_2_schedule_retry): notify alert (manual booking needed)
