Build the Director Bridge stub: TypeScript + Fastify v5 service on Lenovo1
that re-verifies OpenAI webhook signatures (defense-in-depth per D-18),
writes structured JSONL audit log, exposes /health, runs HTTP-canary

(NOT ICMP — explicit choice with rationale), throttles Discord ALERTs to

it, signed webhooks land in the void (forwarder gets 502 forever). The
heartbeat is the operational signal that the entire WG-tunneled control
plane is healthy — Phase 2+ will piggy-back on this when /accept latency
becomes critical. Phase 1 STUB does NOT call OpenAI /accept (that is
explicitly Phase 2 per D-07).

Output: Service running, /health green, /webhook accepts signed POSTs and
appends JSONL log entry per request, heartbeat alerts on simulated WG drop,
systemd restarts within 5s of kill -9.
