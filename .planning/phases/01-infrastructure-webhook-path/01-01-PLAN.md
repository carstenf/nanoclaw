<objective>
Pin WireGuard MTU to 1380 on both peers (Lenovo1 + Hetzner) per D-14 to prevent
PMTU-blackhole on tunneled webhook traffic, and add a Hetzner cloud-firewall
rule blocking inbound 9876/tcp so the forwarder's `network_mode: host` binding
does not expose the webhook port to the public internet (Pitfall NEW-1).

Purpose: Eliminate the two infrastructure foot-guns identified in RESEARCH.md
before any service consumes the tunnel. WG MTU mismatch silently truncates
>1380-byte payloads (e.g. realtime.call.incoming with full SIP headers); the
firewall gap lets internet attackers bypass Caddy and pound the forwarder
directly.

Output: Both wg0.conf files contain `MTU = 1380`, both interfaces show
`mtu 1380` at runtime, MTU probe verifies (success at 1352 AND fail at 1500 —
both halves are required to PROVE the blackhole boundary), and 9876/tcp is
unreachable from the public internet.
</objective>
