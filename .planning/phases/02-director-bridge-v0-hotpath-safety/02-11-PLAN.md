| T-02-11-05 | Tool-Args-PII in bridge-JSONL | mitigate | JSONL enthaelt nur tool_name + latency + status + bytes_out, keine args/result. |
| T-02-11-06 | Unauthorized-Dispatch | mitigate | Bestehende Allowlist (02-07) + schema validation. Nur whitelisted Tools kommen durch. |
