<objective>
Fix Defect #3 (HIGH): upgrade the Realtime session's ASR model from whisper-1 to gpt-4o-mini-transcribe. whisper-1 at 8kHz telephony bandwidth produced structurally poor German output even with language='de' pinned (DEFECTS.md §3: "Hallo, hier Restaurant Bellavista" → "Jan-Uwe das war es von Bellevista"). The AMD classifier's transcript-cue regex (CASE2_MAILBOX_CUE_REGEX_V2) cannot match on garbled transcripts, and the AMD model receives corrupted user input that may confound classification.

gpt-4o-mini-transcribe is a drop-in replacement (RESEARCH §3.5 — we consume only `.completed` events, not `.delta` streaming events) with substantially lower WER on FLEURS German and is the documented 2026 OpenAI Realtime best-practice for German telephony audio.

Purpose: Unblock C2-07 (AMD verdict reliability depends on quality transcripts for the transcript-cue path) and QUAL-01 (live PSTN test requires intelligible transcription for verification).

Output: config.ts transcription.model literal changed from 'whisper-1' to 'gpt-4o-mini-transcribe'; regression test guards the value.
